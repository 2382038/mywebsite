Hello, my name is Aimee Kayla Miracle Simbolon, i want tell you about my holiday during the summer break. This past summer, my sister Angie and I went to visit our family in the United States. While we were there, I spent a lot of time with my uncle, aunt, and cousins. I also got to meet up with some of my friends and relatives who have already settled in the USA. When I landed at LAX, aside from being nervous about the immigration, I was also worried about the time difference. I was afraid it would take me a while to adjust, but surprisingly, I adapted quickly. I only experienced jet lag for two days. On the first day, I rested, and the next morning, my uncle took me hiking, which helped me adjust, so after those two days, I was back on track.

During the holiday, I did many activities, including visiting various famous tourist spots like the Hollywood Sign, Beverly Hills, Palm Springs, and more. The first week was filled with excitement. I was not only reunited with my close family but also met my aunties family who had already settled there. We spent almost a week together in San Diego, staying together, eating together, and having a great time. I also made many new friends at church, who were very welcoming and made me feel at home. During my time in America, I was thrilled to achieve two of my dreams: visiting Disneyland and attending a concert by either the Heritage Singers, Gaither, or The Collingsworth Family. Just two days before I returned home, I had the incredible opportunity to attend a Heritage Singers concert. I am so grateful for the chance to vacation in the USA last summer.

However, at the beginning of August, I had to return to Indonesia to continue my studies. I had to attend campus orientation and then register for the new semester. In the end, I had a fantastic holiday, gaining many new experiences and enjoying my time with loved ones.
